IdiomLingo Privacy Policy

App and Developer

IdiomLingo is a mobile app for learning English idioms.

Developer: Serghei Muzicica (ms82708@gmail.com)

What Data We Collect

We do not collect or store personal data on our servers. The app does not require:

  • User accounts, sign-in, or registration
  • Your name, email, or phone number
  • Location access
  • Contact lists or device identifiers

The app stores learning progress and settings locally on your device only using AsyncStorage.

How Data Is Used

The app uses local storage (AsyncStorage) to save:

  • Learning progress (which idioms you've learned)
  • Favorite idioms
  • Study streak and simple learning statistics
  • App settings (text size, auto-play audio, etc.)

Your learning data is stored only on your device and is not uploaded by us.

Audio Files

The app uses expo-av for audio playback. Audio files with idiom pronunciations are loaded from a cloud storage CDN (Cloudflare R2) over HTTPS.

When your device requests audio, Cloudflare processes technical data necessary for content delivery and security (such as IP address and request timestamp) according to their privacy information: https://www.cloudflare.com/privacypolicy/

We do not receive, store, or have access to this technical data, and we do not track which specific user plays which audio.

Advertising and Analytics

We have none:

  • No advertising (Google AdMob, banner ads, etc.)
  • No analytics (Google Analytics, Firebase, etc.)
  • No trackers or counters

Data Storage and Security

All data is stored exclusively on your device using Expo/React Native's built-in storage system (AsyncStorage). We don't use cloud servers or external databases.

If you delete the app, all data will be permanently deleted.

Your Rights

You can delete all your data at any time by:

  • Using in-app options (Settings → Clear favorites / Reset all progress)
  • Clearing app data in device settings (Settings → Apps → IdiomLingo → Storage → Clear Data)
  • Uninstalling the app

Third-Party Data Sharing

We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties. The only third-party service involved is Cloudflare (R2) for hosting and delivering audio files, as described above. There is no cloud sync and no external analytics/advertising services.

Your learning data stays on your phone. Audio content is delivered from Cloudflare R2 when you choose to play it.
